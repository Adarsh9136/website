Let's build decentralized applications

Decentralization is gaining momentum. In the coming years, emerging blockchain technologies like Ethereum will aid in distributing power from the center to the edges of our communities. This movement is capable to bring about the most significant technological and social advancements.

This hackathon is for all the developers and promising students who wish to contribute to our decentralized future. For the first time in India, ETHIndia brings together the curious minds, industry experts and companies to leverage and utilise the potential of Blockchain. We strive to bring in developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We’ll try to keep things simple.
